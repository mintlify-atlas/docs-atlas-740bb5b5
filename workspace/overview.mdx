## What is a Workspace?

A workspace (also called an organization) is your team's shared environment in Relio. All contacts, properties, deals, and other CRM data are scoped to a workspace. Each workspace has its own:

- Member list with defined roles and permissions
- Billing and subscription plan
- Custom fields, views, and templates
- Integrations and API tokens
- Data isolation from other workspaces

## Creating a Workspace

When you first sign up for Relio, a workspace is automatically created for you. You can create additional workspaces to separate different teams, companies, or business units.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to workspace settings">
    Click on your workspace name in the top navigation and select "Create new workspace"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter workspace details">
    Provide a name for your workspace. This will be used to generate a unique slug for your workspace URL.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start using your workspace">
    Your new workspace is created with a free plan by default. You can invite team members and upgrade your plan as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Switching Between Workspaces

If you're a member of multiple workspaces, you can easily switch between them:

1. Click on your current workspace name in the top navigation
2. Select the workspace you want to switch to from the dropdown
3. The page will reload with the selected workspace as your active workspace

Your active workspace is stored in your session and persists across browser sessions.

## Workspace Settings

Workspace settings allow you to configure your organization's preferences and behavior. Access workspace settings by navigating to **Settings → Workspace**.

### General Settings

- **Workspace name**: Update your workspace's display name
- **Workspace slug**: Your unique URL identifier (e.g., `app.relio.com/your-slug`)
- **Workspace deletion**: Permanently delete your workspace and all associated data

<Warning>
  Deleting a workspace is **permanent and irreversible**. All contacts, properties, deals, activities, files, and other data will be permanently deleted. You must type your workspace name exactly to confirm deletion.
</Warning>

### Available Configuration Areas

From workspace settings, you can access:

- **[Members](/workspace/members)** - Manage team members, roles, and invitations
- **[Billing](/workspace/billing)** - View plan details, upgrade, and manage subscriptions
- **[Integrations](/workspace/integrations)** - Connect third-party services and data providers
- **API Tokens** - Generate API tokens for programmatic access
- **Webhooks** - Configure webhooks for real-time event notifications

## Technical Details

For developers and technical users:

- All API requests are scoped to the active workspace via the `activeOrganizationId` session field
- Workspace procedures enforce membership validation and role-based permissions
- Data isolation is enforced at the database query level with `workspaceId` filters
- See `packages/api/src/index.ts` for procedure definitions: `workspaceProcedure`, `adminProcedure`, `ownerProcedure`
