## Overview

Manage your team by inviting members, assigning roles, and controlling access to workspace features. Member management is available to workspace owners and admins.

## Member Roles

Relio has three workspace roles with different permission levels:

### Owner

The owner has full control over the workspace. There is one owner per workspace.

**Permissions:**
- All admin permissions
- Manage workspace billing and subscriptions
- Delete the workspace
- Transfer ownership (coming soon)

**API Reference:** `ownerProcedure` in `packages/api/src/index.ts:297`

### Admin

Admins can manage workspace configuration and members.

**Permissions:**
- All member permissions
- Delete records (contacts, properties, companies, deals)
- Manage custom fields, email templates, and views
- Invite and remove team members
- Manage workspace integrations
- Access API tokens and webhooks

**API Reference:** `adminProcedure` in `packages/api/src/index.ts:281`

### Member

Members have read and write access to CRM data.

**Permissions:**
- View and create contacts, properties, companies, and deals
- Create and edit activities, tasks, and notes
- Upload and manage files
- Use AI features (subject to workspace quota)
- View workspace insights and dashboards

**API Reference:** `workspaceProcedure` in `packages/api/src/index.ts:148`

<Warning>
  Only **owners** can manage billing. Members and admins cannot upgrade plans, change seat limits, or access payment settings.
</Warning>

## Inviting Team Members

Invite new members to your workspace via email invitation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to member settings">
    Go to **Settings → Workspace → Members**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Invite Member'">
    Enter the email address of the person you want to invite
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select a role">
    Choose whether the new member should be a **Member** or **Admin**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send invitation">
    Click **Send Invitation**. The recipient will receive an email with a link to join your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Permissions

You can change a member's role at any time:

1. Navigate to **Settings → Workspace → Members**
2. Find the member in the list
3. Click the role dropdown next to their name
4. Select the new role

Role changes take effect immediately. If you demote an admin to a member, they will lose access to admin-only features like custom field management and member invitations.

## Removing Members

To remove a member from your workspace:

1. Navigate to **Settings → Workspace → Members**
2. Find the member in the list
3. Click the **Remove** button next to their name
4. Confirm the removal

Removing a member:
- Immediately revokes their access to the workspace
- Does not delete their data or records they created
- Frees up a seat on your plan
- Cannot be undone (you must re-invite them if needed)

## Seat Limits

Your workspace plan determines how many members can be active:

- **Free plan**: 1 seat
- **Pro plan**: Varies by subscription (seat-based pricing)

<Warning>
  If your workspace exceeds its seat limit on the free plan, all members will be locked out until you:
  - Remove members to get back under the limit
  - Start a free trial
  - Upgrade to a Pro plan
  
  Only billing and role management features remain accessible during a seat limit lockout.
</Warning>

Super admins (platform-level users) do not consume a seat and are excluded from seat limit enforcement.

## Technical Details

For developers:

- Member roles are stored in the `member` table with `role` column: `"owner"`, `"admin"`, or `"member"`
- Role checks are enforced via middleware: `workspaceProcedure`, `adminProcedure`, `ownerProcedure`
- Seat limit enforcement happens in `workspaceProcedure` (see `packages/api/src/index.ts:217-256`)
- Super admins (`user.role = "super_admin"`) bypass seat limits entirely
