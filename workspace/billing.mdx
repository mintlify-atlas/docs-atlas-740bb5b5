## Overview

Relio offers flexible pricing with a free plan and paid Pro plans. All billing operations require the **owner** role.

## Plan Tiers

### Free Plan

- **Seats**: 1 user
- **Rate limits**: 180 requests/minute (general), 30 mutations/minute
- **AI quota**: Limited monthly messages
- **Features**: Full CRM functionality with usage limits

### Pro Plan

Available on monthly or yearly billing cycles.

- **Seats**: Configurable (minimum based on current team size)
- **Rate limits**: 600 requests/minute (general), 150 mutations/minute
- **AI quota**: Generous monthly allowance (scales with seats)
- **Features**: Priority support, advanced integrations, higher API limits

<Warning>
  **Trial Period**: New Pro subscriptions include a **14-day free trial**. You will not be charged until the trial ends. Only one trial per workspace is allowed.
</Warning>

## Upgrading Your Plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to billing settings">
    Go to **Settings → Workspace → Billing**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Pro plan">
    Click **Upgrade to Pro** and choose your billing cycle (monthly or yearly)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure seats">
    Choose the number of seats you need. The minimum is set to your current active member count.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    You'll be redirected to a secure Polar checkout page to enter payment details.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    After successful payment, you'll return to Relio with Pro access activated immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Seat-Based Pricing

Relio Pro uses seat-based pricing when enabled by your administrator.

- Each active member (excluding super admins) consumes one seat
- You can add or remove seats at any time via the customer portal
- Seat changes are prorated automatically
- Your subscription will be adjusted at your next billing cycle

**Fallback Behavior**: If seat-based pricing is not enabled, Pro plans default to a fixed seat limit (configured via `POLAR_PRO_FALLBACK_SEAT_LIMIT`).

## Managing Your Subscription

Once subscribed, you can manage your plan through the **Polar Customer Portal**:

1. Go to **Settings → Workspace → Billing**
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. You'll be redirected to the Polar portal where you can:
   - Update payment method
   - Add or remove seats
   - View invoices and payment history
   - Cancel your subscription

## Downgrading or Canceling

To downgrade from Pro to Free:

1. Access the Polar customer portal via **Manage Subscription**
2. Cancel your subscription
3. Your Pro access will remain active until the end of your billing period
4. After cancellation, your workspace will revert to the Free plan

<Warning>
  If you cancel Pro and your active member count exceeds the free plan limit (1 seat), your workspace will be **locked** until you:
  - Remove members to get back under the limit
  - Re-subscribe to Pro
  - Request a trial extension (one-time, 7 days)
</Warning>

## Trial Extensions

If your Pro trial has ended and you need more time to evaluate Relio:

1. Go to **Settings → Workspace → Billing**
2. Click **Request Trial Extension**
3. You'll receive a **one-time 7-day extension** with full Pro access

**Restrictions:**
- Only available if you've used a trial before
- Can only be used once per workspace
- Not available if you currently have an active Pro subscription

See `packages/api/src/routers/billing.ts:449` for implementation details.

## AI Usage & Top-Ups

Relio includes AI-powered features with monthly quota limits:

- **Free plan**: Base quota for AI chat messages
- **Pro plan**: Generous quota that scales with your seat count

If you exceed your monthly AI quota, you can purchase top-up packs:

- **Starter Pack**: Additional messages for occasional use
- **Standard Pack**: Medium-sized top-up
- **Bulk Pack**: Large quota increase for power users

Top-ups are one-time purchases and do not auto-renew.

## Payment Management

All payments are processed securely through **Polar**. Relio never stores your credit card information.

- **Accepted payment methods**: Major credit/debit cards
- **Billing cycle**: Monthly or yearly
- **Automatic renewal**: Subscriptions auto-renew unless canceled
- **Invoices**: Available in the Polar customer portal

## Billing Status Reference

Your workspace can have one of the following billing statuses:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| `free` | No active subscription |
| `trialing` | In 14-day Pro trial period |
| `active` | Paid Pro subscription active |
| `past_due` | Payment failed, grace period active |
| `canceled` | Subscription canceled, Pro access expired |

See `packages/api/src/lib/billing.ts:9` for the type definitions.

## Technical Details

For developers and technical users:

- Billing data is stored in the `workspaceBilling` table
- Polar is used as the billing provider (Polar SDK integration)
- Seat limits are enforced in `workspaceProcedure` middleware
- Rate limits are determined by `planTier` and enforced per workspace
- Workspace external IDs use the format: `workspace:{organizationId}`

**Environment Variables:**
- `POLAR_ORGANIZATION_TOKEN`: API token for Polar integration
- `POLAR_PRO_MONTHLY_PRODUCT_ID`: Product ID for monthly Pro plan
- `POLAR_PRO_YEARLY_PRODUCT_ID`: Product ID for yearly Pro plan
- `POLAR_SEAT_BASED_PRICING_ENABLED`: Enable seat-based pricing (`"true"` or `"false"`)
- `POLAR_PRO_FALLBACK_SEAT_LIMIT`: Default seat limit when seat-based pricing is disabled
