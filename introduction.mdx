# Welcome to Relio

Relio is a comprehensive CRM platform designed from the ground up for real estate professionals. Whether you're an individual agent, part of a team, or running an agency, Relio helps you manage your entire real estate business in one place.

## What is Relio?

Relio is a full-featured Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system that brings together all aspects of your real estate business:

- **Contact & Company Management** - Keep detailed records of buyers, sellers, investors, and business contacts
- **Property Records** - Track properties with comprehensive data including MLS listings, county assessor information, and enrichment data
- **Deal Pipeline Management** - Move deals through customizable pipelines from canvassing to closing
- **Real-Time Collaboration** - See who's viewing records, editing fields, and typing in real-time with presence indicators
- **AI-Powered Analytics** - Get intelligent insights about your contacts, properties, and market trends
- **Multi-Platform Access** - Work seamlessly across web, mobile (iOS/Android), and desktop (Windows/Mac/Linux)

## Key Features

### Contact & Company Management

Manage all your relationships in one place with rich contact profiles that include:
- Multiple emails, phone numbers, and addresses
- Social media links and professional networks
- Connection strength scoring and relationship tracking
- Buyer needs and preferences with property criteria
- Activity timeline with notes, calls, emails, and tasks

### Property Records

Comprehensive property management with:
- Address autocomplete and geocoding
- MLS integration and property listings
- County assessor data (APN, parcel boundaries, tax history)
- Property enrichment (walk score, crime index, nearby amenities)
- Visual map views with parcel boundaries
- Sale history and permit tracking

### Deal Management

Track your deals with:
- Customizable deal pipelines for different transaction types
- Stage-based workflows (Canvassing → Qualification → LOI → Due Diligence → Closed)
- Deal value tracking and probability estimates
- Expected close dates and timeline views
- Links to contacts, properties, and companies
- Sharing and visibility controls

### Real-Time Collaboration

Work together seamlessly with:
- Online presence indicators showing who's active in your workspace
- Field-level locking when someone is editing
- Live activity feed updates without manual refresh
- Typing indicators in comments and notes
- Grid cell collaboration with colored borders

### AI Analytics

Leverage AI to:
- Get insights about contact relationships and deal potential
- Analyze property data and market trends
- Smart search across all your records
- Automated activity tracking and suggestions

- Keep all your contacts, properties, and deals organized in one place

- Access your CRM from anywhere - office, showing, or home
- Track your pipeline and forecast closings

### For Real Estate Teams

- Centralize all client and property data across your organization
- Track team performance and deal flow
- Maintain data security with role-based permissions
- Scale with workspace seat management and billing controls

## Technology Stack

Relio is built with modern, best-in-class technologies:

- **Frontend**: Next.js (web), React Native with Expo (mobile), Tauri (desktop)
- **Backend**: Hono server with tRPC for type-safe APIs
- **Database**: PostgreSQL with Drizzle ORM
- **Authentication**: Better Auth with organization support
- **Real-time**: Supabase Realtime for collaboration features
- **AI**: Integrated AI SDKs for intelligent insights
