## Creating Companies

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter company name">
    The only required field:
    - `name` - Company name
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add business details">
    Core company information:
    - `domain` - Company website domain (e.g., "relio.com")
    - `website` - Full website URL
    - `email` - General contact email
    - `phone` - Main phone number
    - `type` - Company type (see below)
    - `description` - Company overview
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set location">
    Physical address and location:
    - `address` - Street address
    - `city` - City
    - `state` - State/province
    - `zip` - Postal code
    - `latitude`, `longitude` - Auto-populated for mapping
    - `primaryLocation` - Primary market/region
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Link social profiles">
    Add social media and professional profiles:
    - `linkedIn` - LinkedIn company page
    - `twitter` - Twitter/X handle
    - `facebook` - Facebook page
    - `instagram` - Instagram handle
    - `angelList` - AngelList profile (for startups)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Company Types

Categorize companies by their primary business:

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `brokerage` | Real estate brokerage firms |
| `developer` | Property developers and builders |
| `property_management` | Property management companies |
| `investment_firm` | Real estate investment firms and funds |
| `title` | Title and escrow companies |
| `lender` | Mortgage lenders and banks |
| `other` | Other business types |

<Info>
  The `type` field helps filter and segment companies for targeted outreach and reporting.
</Info>

## Company Fields Reference

### Core Information

| Field | Type | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| `name` | text | Company name (required) |
| `domain` | text | Website domain |
| `logoUrl` | text | Company logo URL |
| `type` | enum | Company type (see above) |
| `description` | text | Company description |
| `website` | text | Company website URL |

### Contact Information

| Field | Type | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| `phone` | text | Main phone number |
| `email` | text | General contact email |
| `address` | text | Street address |
| `city` | text | City |
| `state` | text | State/province |
| `zip` | text | Postal code |

| Field | Type | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|

### Business Intelligence (Enriched)

These fields are typically populated through automatic enrichment:

| Field | Type | Values/Description |
|-------|------|-------------------|
| `employeeRange` | enum | `1-10`, `11-50`, `51-200`, `201-500`, `501-1000`, `1001-5000`, `5001-10000`, `10001+` |
| `estimatedArr` | enum | `pre_revenue`, `under_1m`, `1m_10m`, `10m_50m`, `50m_100m`, `100m_500m`, `500m_1b`, `over_1b` |
| `fundingRaised` | integer | Total funding raised (dollars) |
| `foundationDate` | timestamp | Company founding date |
| `categories` | jsonb | Array of business categories/tags |

<Tip>
  Enrichment data helps prioritize high-value prospects and understand company capabilities.
</Tip>

### Enrichment Tracking

| Field | Type | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| `lastEnrichedAt` | timestamp | Last enrichment date |

## Linking Contacts to Companies

Associate employees, executives, and key contacts with companies:

1. Open a contact record
2. Use the company relationship feature to link them
3. Optionally specify their role: `employee`, `owner`, `partner`, `client`

<Note>
  When you link a contact to a company, you can view all associated contacts from the company's detail view.
</Note>

## Company-Property Relationships

Link companies to properties they:
- Own or manage
- Have listed for sale
- Developed or built
- Are acquiring or selling

Relationship types: `listed_by`, `managed_by`, `developed_by`, `owns`

## Filtering Companies

Companies are indexed for efficient filtering:
- **By workspace** - All queries scoped to your organization
- **By type** - Filter by brokerage, developer, etc.

## Company Enrichment

Relio automatically enriches company records with public business data:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Company Profile" icon="id-card">
    Logo, description, website, and social profiles
  </Card>
  <Card title="Size & Scale" icon="chart-line">
    Employee count ranges and revenue estimates
  </Card>
  <Card title="Funding Data" icon="dollar-sign">
    Total funding raised and investor information
  </Card>
  <Card title="Categories" icon="tags">
    Business categories and industry classifications
  </Card>

Enrichment triggers when:
- A company domain is provided
- The company name matches a known business
- A LinkedIn profile is available

<Info>
  The `lastEnrichedAt` field shows when data was last updated. Re-enrichment occurs periodically to keep data fresh.
</Info>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Always add the company domain">
    Providing the `domain` field enables automatic enrichment and logo fetching. Use the root domain (e.g., "company.com") without "www" or protocols.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Link related contacts">
    Connect employees and key contacts to companies to map organizational relationships and identify decision-makers.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use company types strategically">
    Accurate `type` classification enables targeted filtering for marketing, reporting, and relationship management.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Monitor enrichment status">
    Check `lastEnrichedAt` to ensure company data is current. Request re-enrichment for stale records.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
